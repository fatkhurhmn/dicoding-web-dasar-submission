About Me

Hello, my name is Muhammad Fatkhu Rohman, I live in a rural area in Karanganyar Solo. Now I'm still studying at Diponegoro University, majoring in Informatics. I really like programming and design. I think learning programming is fun because it sharpens the brain to solve a problem. Likewise with design, it helps the brain to think creatively and bring out all that it has. Sometimes when I have free time I also do sports, such as football, badminton, cycling, and others. Exercise makes the body healthier and less susceptible to disease. One day I want to be an android developer, besides that I also have a dream to set up a startup.

Education

After graduating from high school, I continued my education in college. Alhamdulillah, I passed the SBMPTN selection and entered the Department of Informatics, Diponegoro University. Since high school I really wanted to go into this major because I like programming. Now that I am in semester 6, I have studied many subjects and applied them. One of my interests is Android Application Development. Since semester 4 I started learning android programming, until I joined the IDCamp scholarship and I choose the Android learning path. In this 6th semester I also took part in the Studi Independen program from the Kampus Merdeka. The program I choose was Bangkit Academy, which offers 3 study paths and I choose the Android learning path as well.
